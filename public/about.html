About the island of Taniti

Experience the island

Experience Nature

Taniti is a small tropical island in the Pacific while the island has less than 500 mi.², the terrain is varied and includes both Sandy and Rocky beaches, a small, safe harbor, rainforest, and a mountainous interior that includes a small active volcano.

Indoor Experiences

Meriton Landing, located on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay is rapidly developing and is home to numerous venues and indoor activities. Meriton Landing is extremely walkable and is open all day and most places are open most of the night. Enjoy!

Outdoor Experiences

Most people visit Taniti to enjoy the beaches, explore the rainforest, or visit the volcano. There are other guided activities visitors may partake in from snorkeling to our tours. There’s something for everyone.

Meet the Locals

Taniti has an indigenous population of about 20,000 until a recent increase in tourism. Most of the Tanitian economy was dominated by fishing or agriculture. Come explore the culture, native architecture, and markets our locals have to offer.
