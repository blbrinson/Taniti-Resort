Taniti has an indigenous population of about 20,000. Until a recent increase in tourism, most of the Tanitian economy was dominated by fishing or agriculture. Come explore the culture, native architecture, and markets our locals have to offer.

Taniti is a small, tropical island in the Pacific. While the island has less than 500 sq mi., the terrain is varied and includes both sandy and rocky beaches, a small, but safe harbor, rainforests, and a mountainous interior that includes a small, active volcano!
