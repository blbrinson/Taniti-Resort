Lodging

Wind Down, Rest, Explore, Repeat

Hotels and Resorts

Looking for the best place to stay and be close to the most activities? Taniti is home to a wide variety of lodging ranging from an inexpensive hotel, one large four-star resort, and many small family-owned hotels. *

Bread and Breakfast

For when you want to be pampered and spoiled first thing in the morning. Taniti is home to a growing number of bed-and-breakfasts. Perfect for the honeymoon with the love of your life, or yourself. *

Beach Houses

Most people visit to enjoy the beaches, so why not sleep on them…literally. Our agents partner with many services for finding deals on housing close to the day, or nightlife, for that perfect getaway. *

Private Housing

For when you want that extra layer of privacy or are visiting on business on an extended stay and just need that extra “quiet” because, let’s be honest, this island doesn’t sleep! *

* All types of lodging are strictly regulated and regularly inspected by the Tanitian government.
