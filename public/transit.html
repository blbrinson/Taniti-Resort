Transit

Almost all visitors in Taniti arrive by air, though some arrive on small cruise ships that dock in Yellow Leaf Bay for one night per week. Taniti is also served by a small airport that can accommodate small jets and propeller planes.

Rental cars can be purchased from a local rental agency near the airport. get around the island at your own pace on your time.

Many of our visitors stay in Meriton Landing and cruise ships dock right in Yellow Leaf Bay for optimal engagement to the excitement.

Taniti offers many public transit options including busses, taxis, and shuttles. Perfect for large groups, and sightseers. They run from 5 am to 11 pm every day.

For hikers and sightseers visiting the mountain, enjoy a boat tour or a shuttle bus to take you to your destination.

Bikes are welcome and very accessible from several vendors to rent. Please note that helmets are required by law. Be safe!

Taniti City is fairly flat and very walkable. Work off that delicious lunch you just inhaled as you tour a gallery or head to a pub. The choice is yours!
