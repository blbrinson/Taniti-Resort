We look forward to your visit!
Taniti City is fairly flat and very walkable. Work off that delicious lunch you just inhaled as you take a tour of a gallery, or head to a pub. The choice is yours!
For hikers and sightseers visiting the mountain, enjoy a boat tour along the way, or a shuttle bus to take you to your destination.
Many of our visitors stay in Meriton Landing and cruise ships dock right in Yellow Leaf Bay for optimal engagement to the excitement.
Bikes are welcome and very accessible in Taniti. They are available from several vendors to rent. Please note helmets are required by law. Be safe!
Perfect for large groups, and sightseers. Taniti offers many public transit options including buses, taxis and shuttles. They run from 5 am to 11 pm every day.
Rental cars can be purchased from a local rental agency near the airport. Get around the island at your pace on your time.
Almost all visitors in Taniti arrive by air, though some arrive on small cruise ships that dock in Yellow Leaf bay for one night per week. Taniti is also served by a small airport that can accommodate small jets and propeller planes.
